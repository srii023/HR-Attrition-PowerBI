# HR Employee Attrition Dashboard — Power BI

## Overview
An interactive 3-page Power BI dashboard analyzing 
1,470 employee records from the IBM HR Analytics dataset 
to identify key attrition trends and patterns.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query (Data Cleaning)
- DAX Measures
- IBM HR Analytics Dataset (Kaggle)

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 — Overview
- KPI Cards: Total Employees, Active Employees, 
  Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Avg Age
- Attrition by Department (Bar Chart)
- Attrition by Gender (Donut Chart)
- Attrition by Education Field (Column Chart)

### Page 2 — Employee Analysis
- Attrition by Job Role (Bar Chart)
- Attrition by Age Group (Column Chart)
- Income vs Attrition by Job Role (Bar Chart)
- Attrition by Business Travel (Donut Chart)

### Page 3 — Satisfaction & Tenure
- Attrition by Job Satisfaction (Column Chart)
- Attrition by Work Life Balance (Bar Chart)
- Attrition Trend by Tenure (Line Chart)
- Attrition by Environment Satisfaction (Donut Chart)

## Key DAX Measures Created
- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate %
- Active Employees
- Avg Monthly Income
- Avg Satisfaction (Attrited Employees)

## Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is **16.1%**
- **Sales & R&D departments** have highest attrition
- Employees aged **25-34** leave the most
- Employees with **Job Satisfaction rating 1** 
  have highest attrition
- Most attrition happens within **first 2 years**
- Frequent business travelers tend to leave more
